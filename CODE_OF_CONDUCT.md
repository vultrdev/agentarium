# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be useful, direct, and respectful.

We want a contributor culture that is:
- sharp without being toxic
- opinionated without being hostile
- critical of code, not of people
- welcoming to serious builders

## Not acceptable

- harassment
- personal attacks
- bigotry
- doxxing
- bad-faith review behavior
- ego-driven repo vandalism disguised as contribution

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close discussions, or block contributors who make the project worse for everyone else.
